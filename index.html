How TokLens works
№ Guide

Three steps. No login. No watermark. No data kept on our servers.

1
Copy the TikTok video link
Open the TikTok app or website, tap Share on any video, then choose Copy link. You can use the short vt.tiktok.com form or the full URL.
2
Paste it above & press Download
TokLens resolves the video through a clean, watermark-stripping backend. Nothing is logged — the request is stateless and ephemeral.
3
Pick your format
You get three direct download buttons: MP4 (No Watermark), MP4 (HD) and MP3 (Audio only). Tap any to save locally.
4
Preview & share
You can preview the video inline before saving, copy the report, or share TokLens using the buttons at the bottom.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is TokLens free to use?

Yes — TokLens is 100% free with no login required. There are no hidden charges, no subscriptions and no watermarks.

Does TokLens add a watermark to videos?

No. We extract clean MP4 files directly without any TikTok watermark — perfect for editing, reposting or archiving.

Can I download TikTok audio as MP3?

Yes. Every video result includes a separate MP3 audio download in addition to HD and SD MP4 versions.

Is TokLens safe and private?

Yes. We do not store URLs, do not require login and use HTTPS-only connections. Your search history is kept locally on your device only.

Why is the first request slow?

The free backend on Render hibernates when idle. The first request may take 20–40 seconds to wake the server; subsequent requests are instant.

Keyboard shortcuts available?

Ctrl/Cmd + V in input → auto-validate · Enter → download · Shift + D → toggle theme · Shift + 1-4 → switch tabs · Esc → clear input
